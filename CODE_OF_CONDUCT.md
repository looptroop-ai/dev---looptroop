# Code of Conduct

## Our Standards

Participants are expected to communicate respectfully, assume good intent, and keep discussion focused on the work. Helpful behavior includes clear bug reports, constructive technical disagreement, patient review, and respect for maintainer decisions.

Unacceptable behavior includes harassment, threats, personal attacks, sustained off-topic disruption, disclosure of private information, or attempts to pressure maintainers into unsafe release, security, or automation decisions.

## Scope

This code of conduct applies in project spaces, including issues, pull requests, discussions, reviews, and community channels associated with LoopTroop.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove comments, close discussions, restrict participation, or block users when behavior harms the project or its contributors. Security-sensitive or private conduct reports should be sent through a non-public maintainer channel.
